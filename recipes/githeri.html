Githeri

Description

Githeri is a boiled mixture of beans and maize.

Ingredients

  • 3 litres Water
  • 1/4 kg Maize
  • 1/4 kg beans

Instructions

  1. Wash the maize and boil it till it softens
  2. Wash the beans and add them to the maize
  3. Continue boiling till the beans also sotften
  4. Stirr well then serve.
