Ugali

Description

Ugali is one of the main staple food in Kenya. Though it looks like a cake, ugali is no sugary junk food.

It is commonly loved because it's rich in carbohydrates. I consider it an African steroid. One piece of this and you can knock out Tyson, blindfolded!

Ingredients

  • 500ml Water
  • 1/4 kg Maize Flour

Instructions

  1. Heat the water till its boiling point.
  2. Add the flour into the water.
  3. Stirr the flour until it hardens.
  4. Leave for about a minute, and serve with soup or any vegetable
