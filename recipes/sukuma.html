Sukuma Wiki

Description

The rich call it a "salad", the rest of us call it Sukuma wiki, because it fuels us throughout the week. Sukuma wiki (kales), is a vegetable rich in carbohydrates. It is commonly eaten as the main food here in Kenya. We love it because it is oh so sweet and very, very wallet friendly.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Kales leaves (Sukuma wiki)

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the kales into tiny pieces
  2. Chop the onion and the tomatoes
  3. Place about a lid of cooking oil in a pan
  4. Gently add the onions in the pan and fry them till they turn brown
  5. Add the tomatoes, and leave it for for about 40 seconds
  6. Stirr the tomatoes and onions well, then add the chopped sukuma
  7. Cover it and steam it for around two minutes
  8. Stir it well then serve it to any meal.
